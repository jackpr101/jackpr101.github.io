Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 25, 2025

At Jacent Labs Ltd ("we", "us", or "our"), accessible from www.jacentlabs.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Jacent Labs and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and services, specifically through our "Contact Us" forms.

The personal information that we collect depends on the context of your interactions with us and the choices you make. This may include:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, and any other information you choose to provide in the message field of our contact forms.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our website.
  • Communicate with you, including responding to your inquiries regarding our software and services.
  • Send you emails regarding updates or information you have specifically requested.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Formspree to process our contact form submissions. When you submit a form on our website, your data (including your name, email, and message) is processed by Formspree. We recommend reviewing Formspree's Privacy Policy to understand how they handle your data.

4. Log Files and Analytics

Jacent Labs follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

5. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Your Data Protection Rights (GDPR)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA) or the UK, you have certain data protection rights. If you wish to be informed what Personal Information we hold about you and if you want it to be removed from our systems, please contact us.

7. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at contact@jacentlabs.com.
